Benetone Films – Best Place for Movies

Benetone Films is a creative film & TV production and production services company headquartered in Bangkok, and with offices in The US, Europe, India and Myanmar. We have been ranked the leading service provider in Thailand for 10 years in a row (2008-2018).

Benetone Films represents the pinnacle of expertise for film production and production service. Our international and local crew of highly motivated and committed professionals offers the best standards of production services.Benetone Films has provided production services for over a hundred feature films and TV series, as well as a thousand TV commercials and digital videos.The company was founded in Bangkok in 2002 and today with office branches in the US, Europe, India, and Myanmar, Benetone Films exported its expertise beyond the Asian borders and has extended their presence from Bangkok to Hollywood, and every place in between.
